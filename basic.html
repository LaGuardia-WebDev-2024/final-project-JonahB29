The Basics

There are many different sports of gymnastics. The most popular of which is Artistic Gymnastics. With big names in mainstream media, such as Stephen Nedoroscik on DWTS, as well as thesport being a headline event at the Olympics, Artistic Gymnastics is arguably the most popular form of gymnastics. For the purpouses of this webpage we will focus on Artistic Gymnastics. There are 2 types of Artistic Gymnastics, Mens and Womens. Mens and Womens Gymnastics are considered 2 different sports.

Mens Gymnastics

Mens Gymnastics has 6 events:

  1. Floor Exercise
  2. Pommel Horse
  3. Still Rings
  4. Vault
  5. Parallel Bars
  6. High Bar

Womens Gymnastics

Womens Gymnastics has 4 events:

  1. Vault
  2. Uneven Bars
  3. Balance Beam
  4. Floor Exercise
